What is DDoS Protection?

A distributed denial-of-service (DDoS) attack is a malicious attempt to disrupt normal traffic of a targeted server, service, or network by flooding the target with different kinds of requests, overwhelming the server, and disconnecting legitimate users from necessary services. MomentMC DDoS mitigates these kinds of attacks by providing always-on protection that is fully automated and free to customers, adding another layer of protection to your infrastructure on the MomentMC Cloud.
