RULES APPLY TO ALL GAMEMODE!

1. Respect Other Players: Treat all players with respect.

2. No Harassment/Hate Speech: Offensive language, hate speech, harassment, and any form of discrimination will not be tolerated.

3. No Spamming or Advertising: Avoid excessive chat spamming, and please refrain from advertising other servers. Do not post links in chat.

4. Language and Behavior: Avoid excessive swearing or inappropriate behavior. Please speak English only so we can properly moderate chat.

5. Cheating and Hacking: Any form of cheating, hacking, exploiting, or using unauthorized mods is strictly prohibited.

6. No Duping/Bug exploiting: Exploiting duplication glitches or bugs is not allowed. Please report any bugs you find immediately

7. Intentionally lagging the server: Intentionally causing server lag can negatively impact the gameplay experience of others and server performance

8. No IRL trading/ IRL scamming: Real life trading (selling in-game items for real money) is not allowed (Ex. Trading a sword in game for irl money). Agreeing to purchase server store items for in-game currency or items, but then failing to fulfill the agreement, is explicitly forbidden. Likewise, proposing to trade in-game money or items for server store items and then failing to deliver the agreed-upon in-game currency or items after obtaining the store item is also forbidden.

9. Doxxing: Sharing or threatening to share personal information (doxxing) is strictly prohibited.

10. Common Sense: Just because something is not explicitly stated above does not make it ok. You may be punished by a staff member for any actions you take that they feel is inappropriate and is not needed in the server.
